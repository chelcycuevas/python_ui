The first thing you have to do when you create a character is select an alliance. This isnt going to impact your character ability in any way; it only affects the Player-Vs-Player game mode. However, if you did not purchase the 'Any Alliance, Any Race' package in the Crown Store for ESO, you will be limited to the races available for that particular alliance once you choose one.

Most days when I queue for my home campaign in Cyrodiil for PVP, the battlefield looks like it is below. For PVP there are 3 options:

    Battlegrounds
    Imperial City
    Cyrodiil

Battlegrounds is a four man group that competes in different games, such as:

    Chaos Ball -- holding onto a ball that slowly deals damage to you and kills you overtime.
    Domination -- flags appear on the map and your team must control the flag zone.
    Crazy King -- same thing as Domination really; you run to the flag and battle to the death. Whoever is left standing takes control.
    Capture the Relic -- each team has a relic you must steal and return to your own home keep before the other team takes yours. RUN and dont die!
    Deathmatch -- the group with the highest number of kills wins. (everybody's favorite)

Imperial City is flooded with hidden ganker nightblades. Good luck surviving unless you have detection potions or skills, but even then, you will likely be getting killed from behind. I dont like IC much because of this. I prefer up close and personal combat without hiding.

Cyrodiil is the main PVP zone where your alliance competes with the other two for territory. You have to ride your mount all over the place and this can be time consuming. It is not as enjoyable unless you are in a large group, then it is possibly the funnest content in the game. If you queue for Cyrodiil, make sure you have selected a campaign as your home campaign in order to get points allocated for your efforts. You can do this by right clicking on the campaign then selecting 'Home'. Once you reach rank 1 for your campaign period, you will receive 50 transmute cyrstals when the campaign ends. I didnt know about this until recently and wish someone had told me earlier. Transmute crystals are extremely valuable because they allow you to change the trait on your weapons or armor, improving its effectiveness.
