Elder Scrolls Online Build Editor

Welcome to my Elder Scrolls Online web app. This is my first Django project. It is a humble work in progress.

I started playing Elder Scrolls Online in 2017 and slowly fell in love with it. I was never interested in gaming before and was actually quite critical of people that wasted time with it, but after watching some relatives play Skyrim, I really liked the content and became curious. Five years later and look at me -- hooked. As the saying goes, "I was initially planning on being a casual fan but then I thought, why not just let it consume my soul instead?".

This site will provide my thoughts on the different classes, races, and content available in the game. You can navigate to the links in the sidebar to find out more information about Elder Scrolls Online, or you can add a build that you have loved for a character you play. To view existing builds you can click on the 'Builds' link or create one below.
